Join Our Team at Wellcare Physiotherapy

Are you passionate about helping people lead healthier and pain-free lives? At Wellcare Physiotherapy, we are committed to providing exceptional care and innovative solutions to improve the quality of life for our patients. Join a team that values expertise, compassion, and a collaborative approach to healing.

Why Work With Us?

•Dynamic Work Environment: Work alongside experienced professionals in a supportive and patient-focused atmosphere.

•Professional Growth: We invest in your career development through training, workshops, and access to the latest techniques and tools in physiotherapy.

•Cutting-Edge Facilities: Be part of a team that uses advanced technology and evidence-based practices for effective treatment.

•Team Spirit: We foster a culture of collaboration, respect, and mutual learning.

Who We're Looking For

We’re always on the lookout for dedicated and talented individuals who share our vision of delivering world-class physiotherapy services. If you are:
•A licensed physiotherapist with a passion for patient care
• An intern or fresher eager to learn and grow
• An administrative professional who excels in customer service and operations

Current Openings

• Physiotherapists – Experienced and Entry-level
• Rehabilitation Specialists
• Front Office Executives
• Clinical Assistants
