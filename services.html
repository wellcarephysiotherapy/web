Innovative Services & Rehabilitation

The physiotherapy service at Well Care Physiotherapy and Sports Rehabilitation offers a complete range of consultation, examination, and treatment services, including.

Dry needling

Dry needling involves inserting fine needles into myofascial trigger points, tendons, or near nerves to stimulate healing in musculoskeletal conditions

Chiropractic

Chiropractic is a licensed health care profession emphasizing the body’s self-healing ability through manual therapy.

Sports
physiotherapy

Sports physiotherapy focuses on preventing, diagnosing, treating, and rehabilitating sports-related injuries for athletes.

Spinal
manipulation

Spinal manipulation involves hands or devices manipulating spine joints to improve pain and function.

Myofascial
Release

Myofascial Release (MFR) is a safe technique to eliminate pain and restore motion.

Trigger point
release

Trigger point release techniques aim to deactivate or "release" hyperirritable spots, reducing muscle tension.

Postrology

Postrology studies body alignment against gravity, impacting health, movement, and overall well-being.

Ergonomics

Ergonomics is the science of designing the work environment to fit the needs of the human body, with the goal of reducing injury and increasing productivity

Pediatric Rehabilitation

Pediatric Rehabilitation at Mayo Clinic focuses on improving function and quality of life for children
