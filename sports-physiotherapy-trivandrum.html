SPORTS PHYSIOTHERAPY in Trivandrum, Kerala

Who this treatment is for:
Amateur and professional athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone recovering from a sports-related injury aiming to return to peak performance safely.

Conditions Treated:
  • Ligament sprains (ACL, MCL)
  • Muscle strains and tears
  • Tendinitis and repetitive strain injuries
  • Dislocations and fractures
  • Plantar fasciitis
Step-by-Step Treatment Process:
  1. Detailed biomechanical assessment to identify the root cause.
  2. Acute pain management using modalities like ice, heat, or ultrasound.
  3. Targeted strengthening, flexibility, and agility drills tailored to your sport.

Expected Recovery Timeline:
Recovery varies from a few weeks for minor sprains to several months for post-surgical rehab (like ACL reconstruction).

Why Choose Wellcare Physiotherapy?

Our sports physiotherapists in Trivandrum have extensive experience working with athletes, offering sport-specific rehab that focuses on both recovery and future injury prevention.
