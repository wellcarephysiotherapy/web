At Wellcare Physiotherapy, we offer a specialized Postpartum & Pelvic Health Recovery Program designed to help women regain strength, confidence, and control after childbirth.

Pregnancy and delivery place significant stress on your body—especially the pelvic floor and core muscles. Many women continue to live with symptoms like pain, weakness, or leakage, assuming it’s normal.

The truth is: these conditions are treatable with the right physiotherapy care.

Why Postpartum
Recovery is Important

After delivery (normal or C-section), your body goes through:

  • Pelvic floor muscle stretching
  • Core muscle weakness
  • Hormonal changes affecting stability

This can lead to:

  • Urinary leakage
  • Back and pelvic pain
  • Abdominal separation (diastasis recti)
  • Weakness and fatigue
  • Poor posture

Without proper rehabilitation, these issues can persist long-term.

The Role of Pelvic
Floor Therapy After Delivery

The pelvic floor muscles support your bladder, uterus, and bowel. During pregnancy and childbirth, these muscles can become weak or dysfunctional.

This may cause:

  • Incontinence (leakage)
  • Pelvic organ prolapse
  • Pain during movement or intercourse
  • Reduced core stability
Pelvic floor therapy is the foundation of safe and complete postpartum recovery.
